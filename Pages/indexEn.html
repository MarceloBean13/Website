About me

My name is Marcelo Feijão born in the great 90's, sort of. I am a big fan of sports, music, cinema and videogames. I have a degree in Physical Education and School Sports and a Post-Graduation in Sports Marketing.
I have been working as a Fitness Instructor the majority of my professional carreer but that scenario has been changing in the last couple of years. My passion for Code Development keeps growing each month and working as a Code Developer becomes more of a reality.
Lately my best friends have been C# and Javascript. I am always ready and willing to learn new skills and tools as I practice everyday to improve my coding abilities.
